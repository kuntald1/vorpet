Under the Hood

AI & Deep Learning
powering the face recognition

Three algorithms working in sequence — from detecting a face in 5ms to confirming identity with a 4096-dimensional fingerprint.

Step 1 — Face Detection
🎯

OpenCV Haar Cascade

Classical computer vision algorithm. Scans the camera frame and detects if a face exists. Very fast — runs every 2.5 seconds.

AlgorithmHaar Cascade XML
OutputBounding box
Speed~5ms
Step 2 — Face Recognition
🧠

VGG-Face Deep CNN

VGGNet architecture trained on 2.6M images of 2,622 identities. Converts a face into a unique 4096-dimensional embedding vector — a mathematical fingerprint.

LibraryDeepFace 0.0.99
Embedding4096-d vector
Speed~200-500ms
Step 3 — Identity Match
📐

Cosine Similarity

Compares two 4096-d face embeddings using cosine similarity formula. Score 0.0–1.0. Threshold 0.60 for kiosk, 0.75 for personal kiosk. Above threshold → identity confirmed.

Formuladot(A,B)/(‖A‖×‖B‖)
Threshold0.60 / 0.75
Output0.0 → 1.0 score

Overtime Management
Auto-detect, Approve & Pay Overtime

AttendPro automatically detects overtime from checkout time vs shift end. Admin configures rates and approves — amounts appear on payslip automatically.

⚙️ Configurable Settings

✓ Weekday rate (default 1.5×)
✓ Weekend rate (default 2.0×)
✓ Basis: Basic or Gross salary
✓ Min threshold (default 30 min)
✓ Max cap per day (default 4 hrs)
✓ Enable/disable toggle

📊 OT Calculation Formula

Hourly Rate = Basic ÷ (Working Days × 8 hrs)
OT Amount = OT Hours × Hourly Rate × Rate Multiplier

Example:
Basic = ₹30,000 | Working Days = 22
Hourly Rate = ₹30,000 ÷ (22×8) = ₹170.45/hr
OT = 3.23 hrs × ₹170.45 × 1.5 = ₹827.03

Loss of Pay Explained
Smart Leave → Salary Integration

When an employee exhausts all paid leaves, AttendPro automatically handles Loss of Pay deduction in the monthly payslip.

1️⃣

Leave Exhausted

Employee has used all 12 Sick Leave days. System shows EXHAUSTED and disables that option in the dropdown.

2️⃣

Choose Loss of Pay

Employee selects Loss of Pay (365 days/yr). Admin approves. The absent days are tracked separately as LOP days.

3️⃣

Automatic Deduction
